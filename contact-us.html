Data Storage & Backup
What would your next step be if your business was destroyed by vandalism or natural disaster? STM IT Support ensures that your disaster recovery plan is more than just a data backup. We’ll create a business continuity plan to make sure your company is up and running as soon as possible.
Email Hosting & Management
STM IT Solutions specializes in the everyday management of your email services, helping you stay focused on your business. We make it simple for your company to communicate by offering reliable, business-class email solutions. If you own a domain name and would like email service, you will need to sign up for an email hosting service. That’s where we come in.
Hosted Business Solutions
For more information about cloud-based computing and how it can benefit your business, allow us to review your IT challenges and deliver the best hosted business solution for your company. You don’t have to be in the same office as your team to work, share and collaborate with one another anymore.
Managed IT Services
We are great with tech support and even better at understanding how businesses work. We care about the growth of your business and we know technology plays an important role in your long-term success. If you’re looking for a IT consultant to tailor a solution for your business, allow us to help.
Virtualization
Virtualization means a lot of different things to a lot of people, but ultimately it means getting the resources of one physical device and dividing it between multiple virtual servers.
Business Application Support
